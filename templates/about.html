About

How it all started....

As the days passed at the hostel, I found a surprising source of inspiration: Tony Stark, better known as Iron Man from the Marvel movies. Stark’s genius, his AI creations, and his futuristic gadgets captured my imagination. During the vacations when I was back home, I decided to take action on this new interest, diving into the world of coding. I chose Python as his first programming language.

Changing Course

But like many journeys, the path was never straightforward. After diving into Python, I faced some challenges, and soon found myself shifting focus. Instead of giving up,I discovered web development—a field that let me combine both creativity and technical know-how in ways that suited me better. This transition opened up new possibilities, and I embraced it fully.

Finally

Determined to make something meaningful out of his experiences,I set out on a project that would combine two of my biggest passions: storytelling and technology. I built a website to share stories from my hostel days and other life experiences. It became a personal project, one that allowed me to express myself, document my growth, and share my journey with the world.
